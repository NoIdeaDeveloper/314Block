# 314Block: My Personal Blocklist Companion

Hello! Welcome to 314Block, my personal blocklist designed to fill in the gaps left by other blocklists. I created this to enhance my own browsing experience, but I thought others might find it useful too!

## What is 314Block?

314Block is a simple, lightweight blocklist that catches those pesky ads and trackers that other blocklists might miss. It's perfect for complementing your existing ad-blocking setup.

## How to Use

1. **Add to Your Blocklist**:
   - For Pi-hole, add the URL to your blocklist configuration.
   - For other ad blockers, follow their specific instructions for adding custom blocklists.

2. **Enjoy a Cleaner Internet**:
   Sit back and enjoy your ad-free, tracker-free browsing experience!
